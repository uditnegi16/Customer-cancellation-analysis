Customer Cancellation Analysis
------------------------------

Key findings:

- Customers on month-to-month contracts are significantly more likely to churn.
- Lack of promotional offers correlates strongly with high churn rates.
- Online security presence decreases the probability of customer churn.
- A higher tenure typically leads to better customer retention.

Proportions:
- No offers churn proportion: 0.2711
- Offer D: Highest retention among all offers.

Visualizations:
- Grouped column chart showed tenure impact on churn/stay.
- Scatter plot displayed relationship between average long-distance charges and monthly revenue.
